What I build

Custom dashboards and lightweight tools that sit on top of the data you already have.

Most small businesses are run from spreadsheets that started simple and gradually became fragile, slow, and hard to understand. I help clean that up and layer a clear, reliable interface on top.

I typically build:

  • Invoice & payment dashboards (who owes you what, and what’s overdue)
  • Operations performance views with weekly snapshots and rankings
  • Inventory / logistics dashboards with service windows and risk indicators
  • Custom Google Sheets–based tools and internal web apps
My background is in operations and continuous improvement, so the focus is not just on charts—it’s on making it easier to run the business day to day, reduce manual work, and make better decisions faster.

How I work

Simple, transparent process designed to move from “messy spreadsheets” to “usable system” in a few weeks.

Step 1
Quick call
You show me your current spreadsheets and talk through what’s painful or confusing about them today.
Step 2
Design & plan
I map out the data model and outline 1–2 dashboards or tools that would give you the clearest view with the least maintenance.
Step 3
Build
I build the dashboards, connect them to your data, and test them using your real workflow and edge cases.
Step 4
Handoff & support
You get a walkthrough, simple documentation, and support while you roll it out and start using it.
