What I build

Custom dashboards and lightweight tools that sit on top of the data you already have.

Most businesses are run from spreadsheets that started simple and gradually became fragile, slow, and hard to understand. I help clean that up and turn it into something you can actually run the business from.

I typically build:

  • Invoice & payment dashboards (who owes you what, and what’s overdue)
  • Operations performance views with weekly snapshots and rankings
  • Inventory / logistics dashboards with service windows and risk indicators
  • Simple internal tools and dashboards that plug into your existing spreadsheets
Because of my operations background, I understand how much of a burden day to day work can be when tools are unclear or time consuming. I focus on creating simple, reliable systems that use the data you already have, free up time, and give owners and managers the visibility they need without asking them to learn new software.

How I work

Simple, transparent process designed to move from “messy spreadsheets” to a usable system in a few weeks, without you needing to learn any new tools.

Step 1
Quick call
You show me your current spreadsheets and talk through what’s painful or confusing about them today. Plain English is perfect, no technical prep needed.
Step 2
Design & plan
I map out how your data should flow and outline 1–2 dashboards or tools that would give you the clearest view with the least maintenance and manual work.
Step 3
Build
I build the dashboards, connect them to your data, and test them using your real workflow and edge cases so they feel natural to use day to day.
Step 4
Handoff & support
You get a walkthrough, simple documentation, and support while you roll it out and start using it with your team.
